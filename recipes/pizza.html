Pizza

Description

Cheese pizza is a timeless classic that showcases the perfect harmony of a crispy, golden crust topped with a rich tomato sauce and a generous layer of melted mozzarella cheese. Simple yet satisfying, this beloved favorite is a go-to choice for pizza lovers of all ages. Whether you're making it for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun weekend treat, cheese pizza is sure to delight with its comforting flavors and irresistible cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano or fresh basil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    2. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well.

  2. Prepare the Dough:

    3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

    Add the Sauce:

    Spread a thin, even layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

    Add the Cheese and Seasonings:

    Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the tomato sauce. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top and add a pinch of dried oregano or fresh basil if desired. You can also sprinkle a small amount of salt for extra flavor.

    Bake the Pizza:

    Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (onto the pizza stone if using) and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade cheese pizza!
